Project - CampusRides

Oct 3, 2019

Many schools are located in small towns. Not everyone on campus has a car. Many such schools implement a shuttle, or jitney service - basically a car service that takes you wherever you like.

At my college, Colby College, if you want to request a ride or a pickup, the current modus operandi is to call the jitney driver or the security department which oversees the jitney service. Isn’t this such an annoying way to request a ride?

We are thus in the process of building an app that allows students in schools who use a “jitney” service to track the jitney’s location and request a ride to and from a location.

Currently under construction for both iOS and Android. Check back soon :-)
