About Allen Ma

Quick facts about myself:

  • Born in China, but grew up in Auckland, New Zealand
  • Junior at Colby College and Dartmouth College studying math and computer science
  • Bilingual in Mandarin and English
  • Enjoys programming, playing sports (soccer, squash, basketball, ping pong), photography, DIY, cooking, and snowboarding

I keep editing my “About” page because I find it very difficult to summarize my life values and philosophies in a few paragraphs. Moreover, these values keep changing as I age and grow.

I enjoy creating new things, learning new concepts, and finding different ways to think about the same idea. I am constantly reminding myself to create instead of consume, and to dive deep instead of skimming the surface. I’ve been told that I am an INTJ, although I’d like to think that personalities are more fluid than 16 buckets of personality types. Nonetheless, I do consider myself to be a driven, detail-oriented invididual. Whatever I decide to pursue, I make it my responsibility to master the craft.

Thank you for taking the time to read this and learn a little bit about what makes me tick.
