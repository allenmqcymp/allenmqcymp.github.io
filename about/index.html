About Me

I am currently studying Computer Science and Math at Colby College, expecting to graduate in 2021. However, I am also studying towards a Bachelor of Electrical Engineering at Dartmouth College, graduating in 2022.

Please note that completing my B.E in 2022 is optional, and that I am open to full-time offers after graduation in 2021.

A little bit about myself:

I am Chinese, although I’ve lived most of my life in Auckland, New Zealand. New Zealand is probably my favourite place to live, although for now I am chilling, quite literally, in New England and hope to explore both the variety of natural landscapes in the US and its rich cultural diversity.

I enjoy programming, woodworking, grasping new ideas (whatever field they may be in) and solving math problems - especially when they are not too tricky and have an elegant solution. I genuinely enjoying creating stuff, and perhaps my life philosophy might be summed up as “bringing satisfaction to others while bringing satisfaction unto yourself”. I’m pretty sure I heard that quote from someone but I don’t remember who, so if you do, lmk.

I also enjoy keeping fit, and do so by being mediocre at a range of sports, including squash, soccer, basketball, and ultimate frisbee. Outdoor-wise, I am a mid-range adrenaline junkie and would not hesitate to shred down a hill on a mountain bike or hit the slopes on a snowboard.

I watch and follow a lot of sports, including rugby, NBA, cricket, squash, and tennis.

In the future, I would like to travel to more countries, and live and work for a while in a non-English speaking country

If you’d like to ask me about my work or about myself, please get in touch :-)
