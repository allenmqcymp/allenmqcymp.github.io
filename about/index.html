About

I am a sophomore studying Computer Science and Math at Colby College.

I am from New Zealand, and though I’ve been told many times that my country is the most beautiful place on Earth, I think every place has its own unique charm. Living and studying in Maine, I’ve explored a few very interesting landscapes, and look forward to exploring many more.

I enjoy programming, woodworking, grasping new ideas (whatever field they may be in) and solving math problems - especially when they are not too tricky and have an elegant solution. I also enjoy keeping fit, and do so by playing ultimate frisbee, lifting weights, hiking, and mountain biking.

If you’d like to ask me about my work or about myself, please get in touch :-)
